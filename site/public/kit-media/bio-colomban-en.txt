COLOMBAN — MEDIA KIT
====================

SHORT BIO (3 lines)
-------------------
Colomban, a committed Merchant Navy officer, entrepreneur and leading figure of the triple maritime transformation — technological, environmental and sociocultural. General Delegate of GASPE, co-founder of VAIATA Dynamics, President of the ENSM Foundation Board.

FULL BIO
--------
A Merchant Navy officer graduated from ENSM (French National Maritime Academy), Colomban sailed specializing in construction vessels, before making the decisive choice to leave the sea in 2017 to have a global impact on the transformation of the maritime sector.

His founding moment dates back to 2013 in Marseille, where he discovered the abyssal technological gap in maritime: environmental data collected monthly on paper, with no reliability whatsoever. This experience forged his conviction that technology and the environment are inseparable in the transformation of the maritime world. Upon returning ashore, he understood that seafarers must be mobilized to drive these evolutions.

Since then, Colomban has championed the triple maritime transition:
- TECHNOLOGICAL: Moving the maritime industry into the post-web digital era, with AI as a critical lever
- ENVIRONMENTAL: Integrating maritime into a sustainable society, beyond climate alone
- SOCIOCULTURAL: Transforming mindsets within and beyond the maritime world, to better radiate its values into society
